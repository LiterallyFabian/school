3 quick facts about bread

What does the Bread Scale mean?

The Bread Scale is a Scientific tool devised by Isaac Newton in 1701 for estimating whether something is bread or not bread. In 1949, Isaac received the Science Nobel Prize for his development of the scale. A popular case where the scale was used is during the moon landing, where Neil Armstrong used it to determinate if the moon was bread or not. Unfortunately, his theory was not right.

Bread usages on the web

Bread is heavliy used in the online world. One classic example is when websites uses breadcrumbs as a navigational aid in UIs and web pages to help the user track and maintain awareness of their locations within programs. Another classic example is the famous Swedish brödtext (breadtext).
